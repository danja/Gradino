"Joy Division - lost live recording"

Yep, as you've no doubt guess, I made it up. For my own ref. as much as anything else I thought I'd write down how.

Some months ago I happened to notice a similarity between "Hello" by Martin Solveig & Dragonette and "Incubation" by Joy Division (that's a much funnier video, btw). I do a bit of recording myself and one particular night I was lacking inspiration so decided to play with those tracks. I tried singing the lyrics of the former over the latter, but it didn't work, the words are a bit too fragmented. But as I'd got everything set up I wondered what else might fit, ideally something as different from Joy Division as possible. Obviously, "Friday" by Rebecca Black.
